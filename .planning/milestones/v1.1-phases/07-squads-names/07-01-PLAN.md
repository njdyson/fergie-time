3. Export `async function fetchNames(count: number, natCode: string): Promise<NameEntry[]>` -- calls `https://randomuser.me/api/?results=${count}&nat=${natCode.toLowerCase()}&inc=name,nat`, parses response, maps to NameEntry[]. Throws on non-OK status.
